# Ride-Booking-Performance-Dashboard.
End-to-End Ride Performance Dashboard using Google Sheets.

# Project Summary:-
This project analyzes ride booking data to understand **Business Performance**, **Customer Behavior**, and **Operational Efficiency.** The analysis is performed using **Google Sheets**, focusing on **KPIs, Pivot Tables, Charts, and Dashboard.**

This project simulates a real-world **ride-hailing company (Uber/Ola style)** use case.

-----

# Dataset Description:-
File: "Raw_Ride_Booking_Data_Analysis.csv"

# Columns Name:
- Booking_ID  
- Booking_Date  
- City  
- Vehicle_Type  
- Booking_Status  
- Fare_Amount  

-----

# Tools and Techniques Used:-
Google Sheets.
- Data Cleaning.
- Pivot Tables.
- Pivot Charts.
- Business KPI Analysis.
- Dashboard Design.

-----

# KPIs Calculated:-
- Total Bookings.
- Total Revenue.
- Completed Rides.
- Cancelled Rides.
- Cancellation Rate (%).
- Average Price per Ride.
- Driver No-Show Rate (%).
- Revenue by City.
- Bookings by Vehicle Type.
- Monthly Revenue Trend.

-----

# Business Objectives:-
- How many bookings are happening?
- How much revenue is generated?
- Which city & vehicle performs best?
- Where are cancellations high?
- Monthly performance trend.
- Operational efficiency.

-----

# Key Analytical Insights:-

- The ride-hailing platform shows a healthy volume of bookings, indicating consistent customer demand across cities.
- A majority of revenue is generated from **completed rides**, highlighting the importance of improving completion rates to maximize revenue.
- **Certain cities contribute a significantly higher share of total revenue**, making them key focus areas for growth and driver allocation.
- Some cities exhibit **higher cancellation and driver no-show rates**, indicating operational inefficiencies and potential demandâ€“supply gaps.
- **Vehicle-type demand varies clearly**, with economy vehicles driving higher booking volume, while premium vehicles contribute higher average fare per ride.
- Monthly booking and revenue trends reveal **seasonal demand patterns**, which can be leveraged for targeted promotions and capacity planning.
